Our Quality Workflow

A disciplined process from raw material to final delivery.

📦

Material Inspection

All incoming raw materials are inspected against Mill Test Certificates (MTC). Dimensions, heat number, and chemical composition are verified before acceptance.

⚙️

Fabrication

Fabrication carried out as per approved drawings by qualified welders. In-process dimensional checks performed at each stage. WPS/PQR compliance maintained throughout.

🔬

NDT Testing

Non-Destructive Testing (UT, RT, MPI) performed on all critical weld joints by ASNT certified technicians. Reports reviewed and accepted before proceeding.

🖌️

Surface Preparation & Painting

Abrasive blasting to Sa 2.5 followed by primer and final coat application as per approved painting scheme. DFT checks at each coat.

🔍

Final Inspection

Pre-dispatch inspection by client and/or third party. Dimensional check, paint DFT, weld review, and documentation package audit completed.

🚚

Dispatch

Secure packaging, proper marking and tagging, and dispatch documentation including packing list, MTC copies, and inspection clearance issued before shipment.
